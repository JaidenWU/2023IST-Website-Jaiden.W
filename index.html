About Bullying

Bullying is a widespread problem that affects individuals of all ages and can have serious consequences on their well-being. It involves repeated aggressive behavior, whether physical, verbal, or relational, that occurs with an imbalance of power between the bully and the victim.

Bullying can take various forms, such as direct physical attacks, spreading rumors, exclusion from social activities, or cyberbullying through online platforms. It can occur in different settings, including schools, workplaces, and communities.

It is important to raise awareness about bullying, understand its impact, and work together to prevent and address it. By providing support, education, and resources, we can create a safe and inclusive environment for everyone.
